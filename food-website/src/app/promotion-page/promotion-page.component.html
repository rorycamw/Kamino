Rancor meat. Need we say more? We take the prime cuts off a Rancor and cure it for upwards of 8-15 months in a spice mixture of our own making. Bantha Steak Burger

A mouth-watering burger, the steak is marinated overnight in a mixture of honey, garlic, soy sauce, and oil secreted from Banthas. The steak is then sous vide until internal temperatures reach 132F before roasting on a hot pan and basting in butter, garlic, and thyme. After roasting, the steak is left resting for 15 minutes before being sliced thinly and combined with vegetables into a burger. Do you like meat? Cause you'll be eating some tonight.
